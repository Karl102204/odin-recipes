Corned beef

DESCRIPTION

Corned beef, or salt beef in the Commonwealth of Nations, is salt-cured brisket of beef. The term comes from the treatment of the meat with large-grained rock salt, also called "corns" of salt.

Ingredients:

  • Canned Corned beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Cooking oil

Steps:

  1. Prepare the pan and put some Cooking oil. Just the right amount as the canned Corned beef comes with its own oil.
  2. Add the Garlic and fry it until some of it are starting to become golden brown.
  3. Before all the Garlic becomes golden brown, add the canned Corned beef.
  4. Wait for all the water from the canned Corned beef evaporates and the it is almost dry.
  5. After the canned Corned beef is almost dry, add the Onions.
  6. You're done! Mix it until how you want your canned Corned beef to be.
