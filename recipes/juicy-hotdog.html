Juicy Hotdog

DESCRIPTION

A hot dog is a food consisting of a grilled or steamed sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun. Many people enjoy this snack even if it is unhealthy as it tastes delicious especially when it is "juicy".

Ingredients:

  • Hotdogs
  • Cooking oil
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cut the hotdogs diagonally to easily cook the hotdogs.
  2. Prepare the pan to use and add some water.
  3. Wait for the water to boil and then add the hotdogs.
  4. Leave the pan under high fire to continue the boil.
  5. Once the water has completely evaporated, add the oil.
  6. Only fry the hotdogs less than 5 minutes in order to avoid the toasting of its skin.
  7. You're done! If you want to serve this with extra juice, do not dry it with paper tissue.
